## 目的

- 谁是 AI Infra 锚
- 谁是核心龙头

- 谁是 follower
- 谁是 recovery candidate
- 谁在掉队

## 评分公式

Composite Leader Score：

```text
25% leadership
+ 18% RS vs QQQ
+ 18% RS vs peer basket
+ 17% revenue visibility
+ 12% margin quality
+ 10% valuation comfort
```

Risk Adjusted Score：

```text
Composite
- 12% event pressure
- 12% crowding risk
- 12% execution risk
```
